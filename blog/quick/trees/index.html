3.13) One Person. One Tree (Trees: Unique Natural Beauty)

Everyone need to plant and take complete care of at least one tree. They need to upload photograph of the same in the space provided in their national portal’s account. Penalties for defaulters: can’t contest in elections, can’t get benefit in reservation policies and in some services/schemes etc. What to do, if something goes wrong even after taking proper care of plant/tree? Write in remarks columns what went wrong and also mention what actions you are going to take to make it up? This is enough to get rid of penalties. Then some people will not take this project seriously!! They just keep on writing remarks. No need to worry. You just start it. With time everything will be okay. With time people will come to know about their responsibilities and importance of plantation and they perform accordingly.

 

The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.
